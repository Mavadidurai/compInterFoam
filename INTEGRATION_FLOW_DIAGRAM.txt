  FIELD OUTPUT FLOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

    enhancedLIFTPhysics generates these fields automatically:

    Phase Explosion Model:
        ├─ explosiveMassSource     [kg/m³/s]  (enhanced ablation rate)
        ├─ explosivePressure       [Pa]        (GPa-scale pressure)
        └─ explosionIndicator      [0-1]       (superheat indicator)

    Plasma Ionization Model:
        ├─ ionizationDegree        [0-1]       (fraction ionized)
        ├─ n_electron              [m⁻³]       (electron density)
        ├─ plasmaPressure          [Pa]        (plasma contribution)
        └─ plasmaShielding         [0-1]       (laser absorption reduction)

    Droplet Breakup Model:
        ├─ WeberNumber             [-]         (instability parameter)
        ├─ breakupIndicator        [0-1]       (breakup intensity)
        ├─ dropletDiameter         [m]         (predicted droplet size)
        └─ breakupRate             [s⁻¹]       (fragmentation frequency)
